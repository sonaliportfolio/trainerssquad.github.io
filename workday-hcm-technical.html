What is Workday HCM Technical Training?

Workday HCM Technical Training is a specialized program focused on the technical aspects of Workday Human Capital Management (HCM). Unlike functional training, which deals with HR processes and business configurations, technical training teaches you how to develop, integrate, and customize Workday HCM solutions using technical tools and frameworks.

The purpose of Workday HCM Technical Training is to equip learners with the skills to develop and customize Workday solutions for complex HR requirements. It enables participants to integrate Workday with other enterprise systems and gain practical experience in technical configuration, reporting, and automation. Additionally, the training prepares individuals for Workday HCM technical consultant roles and helps build a strong foundation for achieving technical certification.
