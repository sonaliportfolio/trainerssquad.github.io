What is Workday HCM Functional Training?

Workday HCM Functional Training focuses on the functional (non-technical) side of Workday. It teaches you how Workday HCM works, how HR processes are configured, and how to support or implement Workday for an organization.

The purpose of Workday HCM Functional Training is to equip learners with a comprehensive understanding of how HR processes operate within Workday. It enables participants to configure and support Workday HCM effectively for organizations while gaining hands-on experience in a real HRIS system. The training also prepares learners for various job roles in the Workday ecosystem and helps build a solid foundation for successfully achieving Workday HCM certification.
