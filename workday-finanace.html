What is Workday Finance Training?

Workday Finance (often “Workday Financial Management” or “Workday Financials”) is a cloud-based enterprise financial-management software. It helps organizations manage core finance functions — such as accounting, payables/receivables, cash/banking, assets, procurement, and financial reporting — on a unified, real-time platform.
