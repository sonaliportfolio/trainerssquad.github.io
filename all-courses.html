Salesforce Training Interview Questions

Let's begin by learning about the top Salesforce interview questions for beginners. These are often asked by hiring managers in case of no to very little experience.

1. What is Salesforce?
Salesforce is a cloud-based Customer Relationship Management (CRM) platform that helps businesses manage sales, marketing, customer service, and operations. It runs fully on the cloud with no hardware/software installations.
2. Why Salesforce is used?
  • Manage customer data efficiently
  • Automate business processes
  • Improve customer service with AI-driven tools
  • Increase sales productivity with forecasting & reporting

ServiceNow Training Interview Questions

Here are the most important ServiceNow interview questions often asked to beginners with little or no experience.

1. What is ServiceNow?
ServiceNow is a cloud-based workflow automation platform that helps organizations manage IT services, HR services, customer operations, and security operations through a centralized system.
2. Why is ServiceNow used?
  • Streamlines IT Service Management (ITSM)
  • Automates workflows across departments
  • Improves incident, problem & change management
  • Provides a single centralized platform for operations

Cloud Computing Training Interview Questions

These are the top Cloud Computing interview questions frequently asked to freshers and beginners.

1. What is Cloud Computing?
Cloud computing is the delivery of computing services—servers, storage, databases, networking, and software—over the internet (“the cloud”) instead of using local machines.
2. What are the types of Cloud?
  • Public Cloud
  • Private Cloud
  • Hybrid Cloud
  • Community Cloud

AWS Training Interview Questions

Here are the most commonly asked AWS interview questions for freshers and beginners with little or no experience.

1. What is AWS?
AWS (Amazon Web Services) is a secure cloud platform that offers computing power, storage, databases, networking, analytics, AI, and more—on a pay-as-you-go model.
2. What services does AWS provide?
  • EC2 (Virtual Servers)
  • S3 (Storage Service)
  • RDS (Managed Databases)
  • Lambda (Serverless Computing)
  • VPC (Networking)

Data Science & AI Training Interview Questions

These are the most commonly asked Data Science & AI interview questions for beginners.

1. What is Data Science?
Data Science involves extracting insights from structured and unstructured data using statistics, machine learning, and analytical techniques.
2. What is Artificial Intelligence?
AI is the ability of machines to mimic human intelligence through learning, reasoning, problem-solving, and decision-making.

Data Analytics & Power BI Training Interview Questions

Frequently asked interview questions for Data Analytics & Power BI beginners.

1. What is Data Analytics?
Data Analytics is the process of analyzing raw data to discover trends, patterns, and insights that support decision-making.
2. What is Power BI?
Power BI is a business analytics tool by Microsoft used to visualize data and create interactive reports and dashboards.

Microsoft Azure Training Interview Questions

These are key Azure interview questions asked to freshers.

1. What is Microsoft Azure?
Azure is Microsoft's cloud platform offering compute, storage, database, AI, analytics, and networking services on a pay-as-you-go model.
2. What are Azure service types?
  • IaaS (Infrastructure as a Service)
  • PaaS (Platform as a Service)
  • SaaS (Software as a Service)

Software Testing Training Interview Questions

These are important Software Testing interview questions for freshers.

1. What is Software Testing?
Software Testing is the process of evaluating an application to ensure it is defect-free and meets the required quality standards.
2. What are types of testing?
  • Manual Testing
  • Automation Testing
  • Functional Testing
  • Non-Functional Testing

Database Training Interview Questions

Important interview questions for Database beginners.

1. What is a Database?
A database is a structured collection of data that allows efficient storage, retrieval, and management using a DBMS.
2. What are types of Databases?
  • Relational Databases (MySQL, PostgreSQL)
  • NoSQL Databases (MongoDB, Cassandra)
  • Cloud Databases (Azure SQL, AWS Aurora)

Blockchain Training Interview Questions

Common Blockchain beginner interview questions.

1. What is Blockchain?
Blockchain is a decentralized, distributed ledger technology that records transactions securely and transparently across multiple nodes.
2. What are key features of Blockchain?
  • Decentralization
  • Transparency
  • Security & Immutability
  • Distributed Ledger System
