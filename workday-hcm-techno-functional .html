What is Workday HCM Techno Functional Training?

Workday HCM Techno-Functional Training is a blended training program that combines both functional and technical aspects of Workday Human Capital Management (HCM). It is designed for professionals who want to understand Workday from end to end — not only how HR processes are configured (functional) but also how integrations, reports, and technical components work (technical).

The purpose of Workday HCM Techno-Functional Training is to help learners gain a 360° understanding of both the functional and technical components of Workday HCM. It equips them to solve HR challenges through system configuration as well as technical solutions, making them capable of handling end-to-end Workday processes. This training prepares learners for roles such as Techno-Functional Consultant, HRIS Analyst, or Integration Analyst, while also building a solid foundation for multiple Workday certifications. Ultimately, it enhances their market value by developing dual expertise that is highly sought after in the industry.
